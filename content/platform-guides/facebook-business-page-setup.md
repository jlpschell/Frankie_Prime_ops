## STEP 6 ‚Äî UPLOAD YOUR PROFILE PICTURE (LOGO)

After the page is created, Facebook will prompt you to upload a profile picture.

1. Click **"Upload a profile picture"**
2. Select your logo file (400x400px PNG)
3. It will show a preview ‚Äî your logo will be cropped to a circle
4. Make sure the important part of your logo is centered
5. Click **Save**

## STEP 7 ‚Äî UPLOAD YOUR COVER PHOTO

1. Click **"Upload a cover photo"** (or click the camera icon on the cover area)
2. Select your cover photo (820x312px)
3. You can drag the image to reposition it
4. Click **Save**

**Don't have a cover photo yet?** Open Canva, search "Facebook Business Page Cover," pick a template, and swap in your colors and tagline. Takes 20 minutes.
