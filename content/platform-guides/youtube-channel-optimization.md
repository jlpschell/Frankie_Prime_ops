## STEP 9 ‚Äî CREATE YOUR CHANNEL TRAILER

A Channel Trailer is the video that auto-plays for non-subscribers who visit your channel. This is your first impression.

**Target length:** 60-90 seconds
**Goal:** Get them to subscribe and book a demo

**Script outline:**
1. **Hook (0-5 sec):** "If you're a contractor and you're missing calls, this channel is for you."
2. **Problem (5-20 sec):** The 47-hour stat. The $18k/month missed revenue math.
3. **Solution (20-45 sec):** What Human Led AI does. Show a quick clip of the AI answering.
4. **Proof (45-70 sec):** What contractors experience after setup.
5. **CTA (70-90 sec):** "Subscribe for weekly tips. And if you want to see a live demo, link is in the description."

**To set your Channel Trailer:**
1. Upload the video
2. In **YouTube Studio** ‚Üí **Customization** ‚Üí **Layout** tab
3. Under **"Channel trailer for people who haven't subscribed"** ‚Üí click **Add**
4. Select your trailer video
5. Click **Publish**

## STEP 11 ‚Äî THUMBNAIL STRATEGY

Your thumbnail is MORE IMPORTANT than your title. People click the image first.

**What works:**
- Your face (if you're willing to be on camera) + bold text
- Split-screen: Problem on left ("MISSED CALL") ‚Üí Solution on right ("BOOKED JOB ‚úì")
- Bold number in large font + short descriptor: **"$18,000/MONTH LOST"**
- Consistent color scheme (your brand colors) so your videos look like a series

**Create in Canva:** 1280x720px ‚Üí search "YouTube thumbnail" ‚Üí pick a bold template
