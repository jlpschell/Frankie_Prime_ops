## FONT PAIRING

### Recommended (both free on Google Fonts)

**Headlines / Display:** `Inter Bold` or `Montserrat Bold`
- Clean, modern, highly readable at any size
- Works on every platform
- Used by thousands of SaaS and professional service companies

**Body / Secondary:** `Inter Regular` or `Open Sans Regular`
- Pairs naturally with the headline font
- Extremely readable on mobile

### In Canva: 
Search "Montserrat" for headlines, "Open Sans" or "Inter" for body. Set these in your Brand Kit so they auto-apply.

### Do NOT use:
- Papyrus, Comic Sans, anything that looks handwritten
- More than 2 fonts total ‚Äî ever

## LOGO DESIGN RULES

### The One Icon Principle
Design ONE icon/logo that works at every size. Do NOT create different logos for different platforms. Your logo must work:
- At 16x16px (favicon ‚Äî tiny, needs to be simple)
- At 180x180px (profile picture)
- At full banner width

**What works at small sizes:** A single letter, a simple geometric shape, or a minimal icon. An "H" with a circuit/wave element. A person silhouette + signal waves. Keep it simple.

### File Formats to Download
When you finalize your logo, export these versions:
- **PNG transparent background** ‚Äî for profile pictures, website
- **PNG white background** ‚Äî for documents
- **JPG** ‚Äî for banners and covers
- **SVG** (if available) ‚Äî scalable, never blurry

### Logo Don'ts
- ‚ùå Don't stretch it ‚Äî ever
- ‚ùå Don't put it on a busy background
- ‚ùå Don't use a color that disappears on white or dark backgrounds
- ‚ùå Don't add drop shadows or gradients in the first version ‚Äî keep it flat and clean

## BRAND SETUP ORDER OF OPERATIONS

1. **Create logo in Canva** (or buy from Looka) ‚Äî 1 hour
2. **Export logo** in all formats (PNG transparent, PNG white bg, JPG)
3. **Build brand kit in Canva** ‚Äî enter colors + fonts, upload logo ‚Äî 15 minutes
4. **Create profile picture** ‚Äî 400x400px, use on every platform ‚Äî 10 minutes
5. **Create Facebook Cover** (820x312) ‚Äî 20 minutes
6. **Use Canva Magic Resize** to auto-resize cover for LinkedIn (1128x191) and YouTube (2560x1440)
7. **Set up each platform** using the individual setup guides in `content/platform-guides/`

**Total time: 3-4 hours for complete brand asset set. Do it once. Do it right.**
