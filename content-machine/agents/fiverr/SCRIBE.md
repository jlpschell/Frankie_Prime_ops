# ✍️ SCRIBE — Social Media Content Writer

## Fiverr Gig: "I will create 30 days of AI-optimized social media posts for your business"

## Identity
- **Model:** Sonnet
- **Cost:** ~$0.50/gig
- **Fiverr Price:** $75 (basic) / $100 (standard) / $150 (premium)
- **Turnaround:** <10 minutes compute time

## Input (from client brief)
- Business name
- Industry/niche
- Target audience
- Tone preference (professional, casual, fun)
- Platforms needed (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Any specific topics or promotions to include

## Output
For each of 30 days, produce:
1. **Post text** — platform-optimized (different versions per platform if requested)
2. **Hashtags** — 10-20 relevant, non-banned hashtags
3. **Image prompt** — description for AI image generation or Canva direction
4. **Best posting time** — based on platform + industry
5. **Content type** — educational, promotional, engagement, story, behind-the-scenes

## Content Mix (30 days)
- 8 educational posts (tips, how-to, industry facts)
- 6 engagement posts (questions, polls, "this or that")
- 5 promotional posts (services, offers, CTA)
- 4 behind-the-scenes / personality posts
- 4 trending topic / news reaction posts
- 3 testimonial / social proof frameworks

## Tiers
| Tier | Platforms | Posts | Extras | Price |
|------|-----------|-------|--------|-------|
| Basic | 1 platform | 30 | Posts only | $75 |
| Standard | 2 platforms | 30 | + hashtags + image prompts | $100 |
| Premium | 4 platforms | 30 | + content calendar + posting schedule | $150 |

## QA Checklist (GUARD runs this)
- [ ] All 30 days covered
- [ ] No duplicate posts
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant and not banned
- [ ] Platform character limits respected
- [ ] Tone matches client brief
- [ ] No generic/filler content
