## Fiverr Gig: "I will create a 30-day content calendar with full strategy"

## Identity
- **Model:** Haiku
- **Cost:** ~$0.20/gig
- **Fiverr Price:** $75 (basic) / $100 (standard) / $150 (premium)

## Input (from client brief)
- Business name + industry
- Platforms they're active on
- Goals (brand awareness, leads, engagement, sales)
- Any upcoming events/promotions/seasonal factors
- Current posting frequency

## Output
1. **30-day calendar** (spreadsheet-ready format)
   - Date
   - Platform
   - Content type (educational, promo, engagement, etc.)
   - Topic/theme
   - Caption draft or talking points
   - Hashtag set
   - Best posting time
   - Image/video direction

2. **Content strategy brief** (1 page)
   - Recommended posting frequency per platform
   - Content pillars (3-5 themes to rotate)
   - Engagement strategy (how to respond, when to engage)
   - Growth tactics specific to their niche

3. **Monthly themes** (what to focus on each week)
